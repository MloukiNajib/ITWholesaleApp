# User Stories

## US1 - Add to Cart
As a customer, I want to be able to add products to my cart to simulate my purchases.

## US2 - Display Total Amount (without discounts)
As a customer, I want to see the total amount of the cart without promotions to know the base price.

## US3 - Apply Promotions
As a system, I want to apply promotional rules based on the day to display a discounted price.

## US4 - Display Stock and Revenue
As an administrator, I want to see the stock status and revenue at the end of each day.
